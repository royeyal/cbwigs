üìä Comparison

‚úÇÔ∏è Overflow Hidden

  • Clean Boundaries: Content stays within container
  • Formal Look: More structured appearance
  • Focus: Attention stays on current slide
  • Navigation: Users rely on buttons/scrollbar
  • Mobile Friendly: No accidental partial slides

üëÅÔ∏è Overflow Visible

  • Preview Effect: Shows upcoming content
  • Engaging: Creates curiosity
  • Context: Users see more slides exist
  • Touch Friendly: Can grab partial slides
  • Modern Look: Contemporary slider design

üéØ Recommendation

Choose based on your design needs:

  • Overflow Hidden: Corporate sites, formal content, clean layouts
  • Overflow Visible: Creative sites, portfolios, engaging experiences
